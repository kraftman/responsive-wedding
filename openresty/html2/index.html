WHY COPENHAGEN?

Copenhagen is the happiest city in the world! If that's not enough for your, its also very pretty, there's loads to do, and its easy to get married there!

WHY TIVOLI?

Tivoli is a victorian amusement park and pleasure garden and is one of the oldest in the world. We really just want a big party for our wedding and Tivoli has everything you need, with rides, food, an aquarium and loads more! It's also right in the heart of copenhagen so we can explore other places too.
